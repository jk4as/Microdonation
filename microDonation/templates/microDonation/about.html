    About Micro Donate

    We make donating the hard way, the easy way

    We're here to help with the little things, so leave your money with someone you can trust

    We started Micro Donate in 2020, in conjunction with Paul McBurney and Mark Sherriff, at the University of Virginia. Our experience showed us a large gap in the online donation software market, and a lack of an all-comphrensive resource to encourage donating and ensure a smooth process. With simplicity built in mind, our services enable you to discover deserving causes across the globe, and allow you to avoid the stress of deciding on a single instituition with our slider method of donating.
